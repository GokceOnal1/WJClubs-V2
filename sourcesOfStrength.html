Sources of Strength
Meets Biweekly Wed during Lunch in room #190 and Student Commons
We meet every Tuesday after school in room G22!

Sources of Strength (SOS) is a nation-wide youth suicide prevention and mental health awareness program. Our purpose is to teach students healthy, evidence-based coping mechanisms to deal with the ups and downs of life, reduce the stigma around mental health, and connect students who are struggling with their mental health to trusted adults. Our goal is to spread hope, help, and strength within the Walter Johnson community.
